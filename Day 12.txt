1.Colors-Change text and border colors

<p style="color: green; border: 2px solid red;">
    This text is green with a red border.
</p>

2.Backgrounds -Set background colors and images

<div style="background-color: lightblue; padding: 20px;">
    This has a light blue background color.
</div>

<div style="background-image: url('https://via.placeholder.com/150'); 
            height: 150px; color: white;">
    Background image applied here.
</div>

3.Fonts-Apply different font families and sizes

<p style="font-family: Arial; font-size: 18px;">
    This text uses Arial font and size 18px.
</p>

<p style="font-family: 'Courier New'; font-size: 22px;">
    This text uses Courier New font and larger size.
</p>

4.Text Styles-Use bold, italic, underline, etc.

<p style="font-weight: bold;">
    This is bold text.
</p>

<p style="font-style: italic;">
    This is italic text.
</p>

<p style="text-decoration: underline;">
    This is underlined text.
</p>
